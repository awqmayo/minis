Suitable for Blood and Plunder, and other pirate skirmish games.

Pirates are an iconic set of historical figures, both within the miniatures world and outside of it. A blend of historical and fantastical elements, pirates are classically characterized with bright colors, and a flair for showbiz. Much like the 17th century landsknechts, pirates favor gaudy clothes, rather than any sort of matching colors or a greater fashion sense.

As such, these figures are both quite enjoyable and thuroughly diffuclt to paint. Due to the lack of pigmentation in most bright paints (Citadel paints are the exception and a lifesaver for reds, oranges and yellows) bright colors are very difficult. In order to paint a brilliant red, one must balance the darkest shadows with maintaing the look of a bright red color, much s wehn painting black one must balance the lightest highlights with the overll very dark color.
